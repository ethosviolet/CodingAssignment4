Jumping Jumbalaya

Jumbalaya

Recipe By: Southern Living

Ingredients

  • 1 pound andoulli sausage, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 2 cup diced sweet onion
  • 1 cup diced celery
  • 1 large red bell pepper, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons Creole seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 (10 oz) cans diced tomatoes and green chiles, drained
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • cups uncooked long-grain rice
  • 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 pound peeled, medium-size raw shrimp, deveined
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  • Garnish: chopped green onions

Directions

  1. Cook sausage in hot oil in a Dutch oven over medium-high heat, stirring constantly, 5 minutes or until browned.
  2. Remove sausage with slotted spoon.
  3. Add diced onions and next 7 ingredients to hot drippings; saut√© 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  4. Stir in tomatoes, next 3 ingredients, and sausage.
  5. Bring to boil over high heat.
  6. Cover, reduce heat to medium, and simmer, stirring occasionally, 20 minutes or until rice is tender.
  7. Stir in shrimp; cover and cook 5 minutes or until shrimp turns pink.
  8. Stir in parsley.
  9. Serve immediately.
